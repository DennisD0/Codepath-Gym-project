Progressive overload is a fundamental principle in strength training. It involves gradually increasing the demands placed on the muscles over time to continually challenge and stimulate growth. This can be achieved by increasing the weight, number of repetitions, or intensity of the exercises.

By consistently pushing your limits and progressively overloading your muscles, you can promote muscle adaptation and growth. This helps prevent plateaus and ensures continuous progress in your fitness journey.
